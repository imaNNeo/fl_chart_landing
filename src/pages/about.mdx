## About FL Chart
**FL Chart** is a free and open-source chart (or data visualization) library for Flutter applications. It's designed to be highly customizable, allowing easy integration of various chart types into [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/) apps.<br />It's maintained by: [**Iman Khoshabi (imaN Neo)**](https://github.com/imaNNeo)

### üëâ Key Features
- **Open-source:** FL Chart is an open-source project in the GitHub, you can take a look at the source code [here](https://github.com/imaNNeo/fl_chart). You are free to fork the repository and modify the source-code to fit your needs.
- **Free to use:** Based on the [LICENSE](https://github.com/imaNNeo/fl_chart/blob/master/LICENSE), FL Chart s completely free for any kind of use (such as personal, commercial, non-commercial and etc)
- **Customizable:** Whether it's [line charts](https://app.flchart.dev/#/line), [bar charts](https://app.flchart.dev/#/bar), [pie charts](https://app.flchart.dev/#/pie), [scatter charts](https://app.flchart.dev/#/scatter), or [radar charts](https://app.flchart.dev/#/radar), FL Chart offers flexibility to fit the style of your app. You can customize many properties of a chart. take a look at our documentation [here](https://github.com/imaNNeo/fl_chart/blob/master/repo_files/documentations/index.md)
- **Multi-platform:** As [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/) is a multi-platform framework which runs everywhere you have pixels (such as `mobile`, `web`, `desktop`, ...) you can use fl_chart on all the platforms that Flutter runs.
