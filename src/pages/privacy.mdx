# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the **FL Chart Showcase App** handles user data. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of our users.

The FL Chart Showcase App does not collect, store, or share any personal information or usage data from its users. This app is solely intended to demonstrate the capabilities of the **FL Chart** library.

## Open-Source Nature

The app is free and open-source, licensed under the [MIT License](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT). You can view the source code on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/imaNNeo/fl_chart).

## No Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics, or advertising platforms. You can use the app without an account or any registration.
