About FL Chart‚Äã

FL Chart is a free and open-source chart (or data visualization) library for Flutter applications. It's designed to be highly customizable, allowing easy integration of various chart types into Flutter apps.
It's maintained by: Iman Khoshabi (imaN Neo)

üëâ Key Features‚Äã

  • Open-source: FL Chart is an open-source project in the GitHub, you can take a look at the source code here. You are free to fork the repository and modify the source-code to fit your needs.
  • Free to use: Based on the LICENSE, FL Chart s completely free for any kind of use (such as personal, commercial, non-commercial and etc)
  • Customizable: Whether it's line charts, bar charts, pie charts, scatter charts, or radar charts, FL Chart offers flexibility to fit the style of your app. You can customize many properties of a chart. take a look at our documentation here
  • Multi-platform: As Flutter is a multi-platform framework which runs everywhere you have pixels (such as mobile, web, desktop, ...) you can use fl_chart on all the platforms that Flutter runs.
