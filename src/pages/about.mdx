## About FL Chart

**FL Chart** is a free and open-source chart library for Flutter applications, developed by Iman Khoshabi (imaNNeo). It's designed to be highly customizable, allowing easy integration of various chart types into Flutter apps.

### Key Features

- **Open-Source:** Completely free and open for use and contributions, FL Chart is a valuable part of the Flutter community.
- **Customizable:** Whether it's line charts, bar charts, pie charts, scatter charts, or radar charts, FL Chart offers flexibility to fit the style of your app.
- **Flutter-Focused:** Built specifically for Flutter, ensuring seamless integration and optimal performance in Flutter apps.
- **Community-Driven:** Continuously evolving with contributions and feedback from developers around the world.
